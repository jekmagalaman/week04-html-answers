The Flash (2023)

A superhero movie directed by Andy Muschietti.

Main Characters

Barry Allen / The Flash

Played by Ezra Miller, Barry Allen is a forensic scientist with super-speed abilities. In this film, he uses his powers to travel back in time, leading to unintended consequences.

Bruce Wayne / Batman

The movie features two versions of Batman—one played by Ben Affleck and another by Michael Keaton. Keaton’s Batman returns to help Barry fix the timeline.

Supergirl

Kara Zor-El, played by Sasha Calle, is introduced as an alternate version of Superman who plays a crucial role in the altered timeline.

Plot Summary

The Flash (2023) follows Barry Allen as he travels back in time to prevent his mother’s murder. However, this decision creates an alternate reality where metahumans don’t exist, General Zod returns, and Bruce Wayne is a different Batman. Barry teams up with his past self, Supergirl, and Michael Keaton’s Batman to restore the timeline and stop Zod.

Setting

Central City

Barry’s home city, where he works as a forensic scientist and fights crime as The Flash.

Alternate Timeline

After Barry alters the past, he finds himself in a timeline without metahumans, making the battle against Zod even more challenging.

Personal Thoughts

a kind of movie you watch with a goofy smile on your face, particularly during Michael Keaton's welcome return as Batman and Sasha Calle's too-brief introduction as Supergirl. But it's ultimately a mixed bag of great moments run aground by a threadbare plot about a multiversal crisis that lacks the emotional heft to support its ambition, leaving me feeling as if the whole story was a bit discombobulated and ungraspable in terms of motivations; even as a teasing look at a summer tentpole movie to come, much of the film looks impressive but struggles to hold up its own story weight, and while a technically proficient and fun offering at the cinema.
